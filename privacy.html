Privacy Policy

Omicentra is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is processed.

1. Data Controller

The website Omicentra operates as an independent informational platform. Contact is available exclusively through the website contact form.

2. Data We Collect

When you use the contact form, the following data may be processed:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

3. Purpose of Processing

Personal data is processed solely for responding to inquiries submitted through the contact form.

4. Legal Basis

Processing is based on Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual communication) or Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to inquiries).

5. Service Providers

The website uses the following infrastructure providers:

  • Cloudflare (hosting and security)
  • Cloudflare Turnstile (bot protection)
  • Resend (email delivery service)

6. Data Retention

Personal data submitted via the contact form is retained only as long as necessary to process and respond to the inquiry.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or marketing cookies. Security-related cookies may be set by infrastructure providers strictly for protection purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data. Such requests may be submitted through the contact form.

9. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the website evolves.
